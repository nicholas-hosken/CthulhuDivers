Have you ever wondered what it would be like to breathe underwater? The Discover Scuba Diving Course is a quick and easy introduction to the world of scuba delivered in the safety of our shallow pool. In the pool you’ll learn the basic safety guidelines and skills needed to dive under the direct supervision of a PADI Professional. Upon completion of the course your instructor will then take you for two open water dives just off the south side of Isla Mujeres.

The first dive will be at Cancun's world famous Underwater Museum where we will visit some of the 470+ sculptures sunk by Jason deCaires Taylor and witness first-hand how the local parrotfish manufacture the beautiful white sand of Cancun’s beaches. On the second dive you’ll get to explore some of the healthiest, most vibrant reefs in the Caribbean such as Manchones Grande, Manchones Chico or Jardines where we will be on the lookout for turtles, barracuda and moray eels!
